# Example prompts (for future MCP + skill)

Use these once tools are connected:

- “How long until my credit cards are paid off if I put an extra $200/month toward debt?”
- “Can I save $10,000 by November given my current categories?”
- “What changed the most in spending compared to last month?”
- “Do I have room for a $400/month subscription after keeping my buffer?”

Always treat answers as **models with assumptions**, not guarantees.
